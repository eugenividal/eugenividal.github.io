Hi there!

I am Eugeni, a transport planner with 10 years of experience from Valencia (Spain) currently enrolled in a 4 years PhD programme at the University of Leeds.

My research project entitled 'Cycling and socioeconomic disadvantage' examines quantitatively socioeconomic inequalities in cycling for transport and leisure, bringing together my professional background (transport planning) and two of my main interests: cycling and social justice. The project is funded by the ESRC and supervised by Dr Robin Lovelace expert in geocomputation, Prof Dr Eva Heinen in travel behaviour and active travel, and Dr Richard Mann in statistical modelling and data analytics.

The PhD programme includes an integrated MSc in Data Analytics and Society over the first two years to provide research students with the foundation skills to complete their research projects. Some of my short-term master's projects can be seen here.

If you have an interest in this project and would like to share ideas or suggestions, please don't hesitate to get in touch with me.
