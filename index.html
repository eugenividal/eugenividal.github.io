Biography

I am a transport planner with 10 years of experience from Valencia (Spain) currently enrolled in a 4 years PhD programme at the University of Leeds.

The PhD programme includes an integrated MSc in Data Analytics and Society over the first two years to provide research students with the foundation skills to complete the research project.

My research project entitled 'Cycling and socioeconomic disadvantage' brings together my personal interests (social justice and cycling) and professional background (transport planning).

The supervisors of the project are Dr Robin Lovelace expert in geocomputation, Dr Eva Heinen in travel behaviour and active travel, and Dr Richard Mann in statistical modelling and data analytics.
