Hi there!


I am an experienced transport planner from Valencia currently enrolled in a 4 years PhD programme at the University of Leeds.

My research project entitled "The correlates of transport modal split: identifying choice and (potential for) change" brings together my personal interests (travel behaviour and active modes) and professional background. The project will answer questions such as: How do people choose their mode of travel? Which determinants are most important? Are these determinants equally important for everyone?; and it will be based on a big data approach.

The PhD programme includes an integrated MSc in Data Analytics and Society over the first two years to provide research students with the foundation skills to complete the research project. In it, I will undertake different short-term projects that I will upload on this web page. The building up of an Agent-Based model (AMB) is already available. Check it out in this link!
